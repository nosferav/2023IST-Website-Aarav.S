What are "minions"?

The minions are small, yellow, cylindrical creatures who have either one or two eyes with goggles and blue overalls impulsive creatures with little self-control, but with a wide-eyed wonder and odd innocence that endears them to viewers and makes them relatable

Summary of major works

The first movie, “Despicable Me”, came out in 2010. The second movie “Despicable Me 2” came out in 2013,　the third movie “Minions” came out in 2015, and the "Despicable Me 3" will be released on July 21.

Creator of "minions"

Pierre Coffin, a French man who co-directed the first two films in the series and the new films, "Minions"

He also did voices in the film for the three main protagonists: Kevin, Stuart and Bob, plus

several hundred other minions as well

He confesses that the minions came about accidentally as he was “doodling a thumbnail cartoon on his children’s attempts at Minion art.”

Comedy techniques used in the minion movies

1) Incongruity

It is the juxtaposition of the unusual with the routine which causes us to laugh at things that surprise us by being different or out of place it is applied to the minions with their unrealistic appearance and their cute but evil actions

2) Slapstick

It is another way that Minions use their fun personalities for comedy with their accidents and their actions Minion movies’ main source of humor and enjoyment comes from the use of non-stop slapstick

3) Puns (banana language)

pun is a type of irony where two words that are similar in sound are deliberately mixed up to poke fun at something even though the banana language is created by the writer of the movies, the use of the funny language creates its own comedy style

Facts

A minion has only three fingers in each hand.

They can survive in outer space as they do not require oxygen to breath and can resist freezing.

They are either one-eyed or two-eyed and always wear metallic goggles for no specific reason.

In Despicable Me, they have slightly crooked teeth but in Despicable Me 2, they have aligned teeth.

They have been licensed by McDonald’s, Hasbro and General Mills.

In Despicable Me, All the minions are voiced by co-director Pierre Coffin.

Technically they are multilingual as their language Minionese is a mix of Spanish, English, Russian, Korean, and Italian etc.
